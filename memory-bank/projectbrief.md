# Project Brief

- Name: Merinda (Scrolli) news/blog magazine site.
- Goal: Publish Turkish-first news, analysis, and magazine content with premium storytelling, strong visuals, and dark-mode-friendly UX.
- Audience: Turkish readers interested in politics, business, tech, culture; both casual and premium subscribers.
- Success criteria: Fast page loads, clear typography hierarchy, responsive layouts, consistent design tokens, and accessible dark mode.
